## The Problem

**Section Title:** Here's what's broken with most training.

### Card 1: Training that people forgot by Friday
Most corporate training is click-and-forget. People sit through it because they have to, retain almost nothing, and go right back to doing things the way they always have. Completion rates look great. Actual behavior change? Not so much.

### Card 2: Expert knowledge trapped in people's heads
Your best performers know things that aren't written down anywhere. When they leave, that knowledge walks out the door with them. Nobody's capturing it, organizing it, or turning it into something the rest of the team can actually use (much less new hires).

### Card 3: Generic content that doesn't sound like you
Off-the-shelf courses and AI-generated content teaches generic skills in a generic voice. They don't reflect your org's real expertise, your culture, or the specific situations your people face every day. Your team deserves better than someone else's template.
